# Day 4 - Flow Builder in Salesforce

## 1. What is Flow Builder?
Flow Builder is Salesforce’s powerful automation tool that allows you to design business processes using a drag-and-drop interface. It enables both admins and developers to automate workflows without writing code, making processes faster, more consistent, and easier to maintain.

## 2. Types of Flows
- **Screen Flow:**  
  A flow that requires user interaction. It displays screens to collect or show information, guiding users step by step.  
  *Example:* A student registration form where users enter details and submit.

- **Record-Triggered Flow:**  
  A flow that runs automatically when a record is created, updated, or deleted.  
  *Example:* Sending a welcome email when a new student record is created.

## 3. My Automation Ideas (5 Examples)
1. Automatically send a welcome email when a new student record is created.  
2. Update a patient’s status to “Admitted” when an admission record is saved.  
3. Assign a case to the correct department based on issue type.  
4. Notify a professor when a student enrolls in their course.  
5. Auto-calculate discounts when an opportunity value exceeds a threshold.

## 4. Manual vs Automated Process
- **Manual Process:** Requires human effort, prone to errors, slower, and inconsistent.  
- **Automated Process:** Runs instantly, reduces errors, saves time, and ensures consistency.

## 5. Reflection: Why Automation Matters in Enterprise Systems
Automation ensures efficiency, accuracy, and scalability. It reduces repetitive tasks, improves customer experience, and allows employees to focus on higher-value work.
